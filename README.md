# Employee Attrition Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing employee data to identify which departments, roles, and employee segments experience the highest attrition, and what factors are driving it.

## Business Problem
Why are we losing employees, and which departments, roles, or employee segments are at the highest attrition risk? This project consolidates employee demographics, compensation, and satisfaction data into a single interactive view to help HR prioritize retention efforts.

## Tools Used
- **Power Query** – data cleaning and transformation
- **Power BI** – data modeling (DAX) and dashboard visualization
- **Dataset:** IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance (Kaggle, fictional dataset), 1,470 employee records

## Dashboard Pages
1. **Overview** – Total Employees, Total Attrition, Avg Monthly Income, and attrition split by department
2. **Attrition Drivers** – Attrition by overtime status, job satisfaction, work-life balance, and income comparison
3. **Department & Role Analysis** – Attrition by job role, age group, marital status, and average tenure by department

## Key Insights
- Total attrition: **[X]** employees out of 1,470 total
- [Department] shows the highest number of employees leaving
- Employees working overtime show notably higher attrition counts than those who don't
- Attrition decreases as job satisfaction and work-life balance scores increase
- [Job Role] has the highest attrition among all roles
